COMMON GOOD ALLIANCE
"Moving the Dollar in Our Community"

Community Voice Line

Question Bank & Call Flow

Mission

Cultivate the social infrastructure for the common good by investing in local assets and business talent, while leveraging the economic power of the Black dollar.

Compassion · Trust · Radical Collaboration

Target Community

North College Hill, Hamilton County, Ohio
Population: ~9,000 residents

Call Flow

Opening
"Thank you for calling the Common Good Alliance Community Voice line. Your input will help shape the future of North College Hill."
Identification
"Please state your full name and the street or neighborhood where you live."
Questions
2-4 questions based on the selected campaign pillar (see below)
Closing
"Thank you for sharing your voice. Your feedback will help the Common Good Alliance advocate for North College Hill. Goodbye."

Strategic Pillars

Land

Strategic acquisition & community ownership

Capital

Investment & economic growth

Arts & Culture

Preserving history & celebrating artists

Housing

Rental & ownership options

Enterprise

Supporting Black-owned businesses

1

LAND Strategic acquisition and utilization

  • "Are there vacant lots or unused properties in your neighborhood that concern you? If so, where?"
    Identifies specific problem areas
  • "What would you like to see built or developed on vacant land in North College Hill?"
    Captures community vision
  • "Would you support community ownership of land - such as a land trust - in your neighborhood? Why or why not?"
    Gauges support for collective models
  • "Is there anything else you'd like to share about land use in North College Hill?"
    Open-ended capture
2

ORGANIZING CAPITAL Investment and economic growth

  • "If you had $500 to invest in a local project, what kind of project would you want to support?"
    Reveals investment priorities
  • "What barriers have you or people you know faced when trying to access loans or funding?"
    Identifies systemic obstacles
  • "Would you participate in a community investment fund to support local Black-owned businesses?"
    Gauges collective action interest
  • "Is there anything else you'd like to share about investment and economic opportunity here?"
    Open-ended capture
3

ARTS & CULTURE Preserving and celebrating local heritage

  • "What local artists, musicians, or cultural traditions in North College Hill should be celebrated or preserved?"
    Surfaces community assets
  • "Where would you like to see public art - such as murals or sculptures - in the neighborhood?"
    Identifies locations for investment
  • "What cultural events or programs would you attend or want to see offered?"
    Gauges demand for programming
  • "Is there anything else you'd like to share about arts and culture in North College Hill?"
    Open-ended capture
4

HOUSING Rental and ownership options

  • "Do you currently rent or own your home?"
    Demographic baseline
  • "What's your biggest challenge with housing right now - affordability, maintenance, safety, or something else?"
    Identifies primary pain points
  • "What would help more residents become homeowners in North College Hill?"
    Surfaces solutions from community
  • "Is there anything else you'd like to share about housing in your neighborhood?"
    Open-ended capture
5

ENTERPRISE Supporting Black-owned businesses

  • "What types of businesses are missing in North College Hill that you wish were here?"
    Identifies market gaps
  • "Do you currently support any Black-owned businesses in the area? Which ones?"
    Maps existing ecosystem
  • "If you're a business owner or aspiring entrepreneur, what support would help you most?"
    Captures entrepreneur needs
  • "Is there anything else you'd like to share about local businesses and economic development?"
    Open-ended capture
